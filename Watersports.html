Watersports around Hayling Island

The home of Windsurfing

Hayling Island is the birthplace of windsurfing. In 1958, when a 12 yr old named Peter Chilvers attached a freely rotating mast and sail to a makeshift board, he introduced the world to a watersport that enthralls thrillseekers of all ages, to this day.
