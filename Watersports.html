Watersports around Hayling Island


The home of Windsurfing


Hayling Island is the birthplace of windsurfing. In 1958, when a 12 yr old named Peter Chilvers attached a freely rotating mast and sail to a makeshift board, he introduced the world to a watersport that enthralls thrill seekers of all ages, to this day.


Watersports for all ages


Not only a centre of excellence for Windsurfing, Hayling Island has a thriving kitesurfing scene, is a haven for dinghy and yacht sailors and has a large body of sit on kayak paddlers. All in all it’s a great watersports location with plenty of on water action ready for the taking.
