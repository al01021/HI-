Situated between Portsmouth and Chichester harbour and linked to the mainland by both road bridge and ferry, Hayling IslandÂ is close enough to offer a fantastic weekend adventure but a world away from your day to day.

Planning your stay

Planning your stay is half the fun, so take time to explore your options, whether getting close to the water through our huge range of watersports on offer, or relaxing on our Blue Flag beaches, Hayling Island is the ultimate destination for thrill-seekers and sun seekers alike. There is also plenty to keep the kids happy and nature lovers will be spoilt for choice given our extensive walks and local wildlife.
