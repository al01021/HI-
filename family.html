Family Day Out

on Hayling Island


Fun for all ages!

There are plenty of things to keep the children happy from Hayling Island’s very own funfair to visiting our Donkey Sanctuary. Of course our Blue Flag beaches offer plenty of scope for the children to entertain themselves whilst you relax with a well-deserved ice cream.

All the fun of the fair..

Kids will love all that Funland Amusement Park has to offer. Whatever the weather there is planty to do. From the breathtaking Mine train roller coaster and Arcades - Family Entertainment Area to a Kidz Kingdom for the little ones.

... and so much more!

For beautiful views of the island’s coastline why not ride Hayling Island’s very own steam train, open on weekends. If you are visiting in May, our annual Donkey Derby festival is for young and old and will be an experience you won’t forget. You can also enjoy our Hayling Pitch and Putt Golf Club.
